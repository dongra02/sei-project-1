Don't Get Poo on Your Shoe!

Don't Get Poo on Your Shoe! follows the same rules as the classic Minesweeper. Only here, the mines were left by a four legged family friend and your job is to identify all of them without getting any on your shoe. You can check a square by clicking on it, but be careful. As soon as you soil your sole, the game is over. You have been provided bags, which you can use by left clicking a patch of grass you believe to contain a pile of digested dog food. Once bagged, you won't be able to uncover the tile. An additional left click will remove the bag, if you decide you made a mistake. The levels progress in lawn size and dog excrement. Choose your level below, and good luck!
